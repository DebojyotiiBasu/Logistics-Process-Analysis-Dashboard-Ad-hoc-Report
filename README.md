# Logistics-Process-Analysis-Dashboard-Ad-hoc-Report
1) Summary - 
End-to-end logistics operations dashboard in Power BI analyzing 27,979 orders, 55 drivers &amp; 45 vehicles across 6 hubs. Tracks delivery performance, delay root causes, vehicle health &amp; customer satisfaction (4.17/5) to drive operational efficiency and reduce 21.12% delay rate.


2) Project Scope - 
a) Designed and developed an end-to-end logistics operations intelligence dashboard in Power BI analyzing 27,979 orders across 6 regional hubs, 55 drivers, and 45 vehicles — translating complex multi-source operational data into structured, executive-ready reports for management decision-making

b) Identified and analyzed errors and inconsistencies across 10 delay categories — pinpointed that Vehicle Breakdown and Road Construction each contributed 623 delayed orders (21.12% overall delay rate) across the network, enabling targeted operational intervention recommendations to streamline budget and reduce delivery failures

c) Used data mining to extract patterns and correlations across driver performance, hub throughput, and vehicle utilization — uncovered that Fort Worth Hub had the highest avg delivery time (36.50 hrs) vs El Paso Hub (35.24 hrs), surfacing actionable hub efficiency gaps for management review

d) Developed ad hoc reporting framework using dynamic slicers and cross-filtering across order status, vehicle type, hub location, and delay reason — enabling management to self-serve answers across 27,979 records without analyst intervention, reducing report turnaround from days to minutes

e) Performed statistical analysis to predict trends in delivery performance and customer satisfaction — tracked Customer Satisfaction Score averaging 4.17/5 across delivered orders and modelled correlation between hub processing time (avg 1.97 hrs) and delay probability to forecast operational improvement targets

f) Recommended process improvements to support budget optimisation by identifying that 12 of 45 vehicles (26.7%) were in active maintenance with avg 11.9 breakdowns per vehicle — built vehicle health monitoring view with maintenance alert flags to proactively reduce breakdown-driven delays and associated operational costs
